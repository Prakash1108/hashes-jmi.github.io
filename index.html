What to expect from HASHes?

HASHes is an initiative started by a 2nd year student from Electronics Engineering to bring together students interested in programming irrespective of department so that there can exist a community and a platform where innovation can be disseminated and protected.

  • Give a platform to incubate ideas

    Sharing and working together on innovative ideas can help them nurture in today's competitive environment. HASHes aims at bringing people together to innovate and participate in innovation…

  • Share and interact with seniors and juniors alike

    With HASHes there can exists an environment where no segregation between juniors and seniors exists leading to a healthy professional relationship among hte students…

  • Development of Alumni and Professional Network

    Considering a dearth of both alumni and professional network in the university and department alike, HASHes aims at building a strong network with them to increase interaction of students especially in the field of computer sciences…
